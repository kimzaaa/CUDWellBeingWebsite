HOME COURT

HomeCourt is an AI-driven app for basketball training that uses a phone's camera to track and analyze shot performance in real time. It provides feedback on accuracy, form, and speed, helping players improve their skills. No extra hardware is needed, making it accessible for individual training or team use.
